How to Become an AURA Reseller

Four simple steps. No hidden fees. No complicated requirements. Just pure earning opportunity.

The 4-Step Reseller Process

1

Message Us to Join

Send us a message on Facebook and say "I want to be a reseller". We'll confirm your details and get you started right away.

2

Get Your FREE Tester

We will give you a free tester kit so you can personally smell, test, and showcase all our scents to your friends and customers. No payment required.

3

Sell & Send Us the Order

Sell at any price you want. When a customer orders from you, message us the order details. We will give you the perfume first so you can personally deliver it to your customer.

4

Deliver & Pay Us Only Then

You give the perfume to your customer and collect your full selling price. That is the only time you pay us — just our fixed original price per bottle. Everything extra is 100% your profit.

🔑 YOUR ADVANTAGE: You never pay us first. You get the perfume → you give it to your customer → you get paid by them → then you pay us our original price. Zero risk for you, always.
