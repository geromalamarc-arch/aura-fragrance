How to use

  1. Edit the Your Cost (confidential reseller price) and Suggested SRP for each scent.
  2. Set Stock Status: In Stock · Low Stock · Out of Stock.
  3. Add an optional Stock Note (e.g. "5 bottles left" or "Restock next week").
  4. Click Save All Changes. That's it!
  5. Open index.html (main site) and refresh — the new prices & stocks appear automatically.
